FAQ

  • What if I don't like one of the sports?

    • That's quite okay. Many triathletes have a sport that they like the least.

  • How did you get started?

    • It has taken me several months just to acclimate to movement. Now that I am nearly ready, the season has closed. Because I have been training relentlessly, I know by the time next season opens, I will be ready for a sprint! My goal is to be able to reach the required distance for each sport and then incorporate what is called brick training. I am taking Meredith Atwood to heart when she said The... journey is about making bold moves and strong decisions, but that does not necessarily mean rushing into goals either.   • Where do I start?

    • It's good to get a baseline for each sport. You can print off a sheet to write down all of your sports stats. Start on your bike and ride for as long as you can, being sure to pace yourself. Record your time and distance. Do the same for all three sports. This will give you a general idea of where you are. Then make every effort to get better with each practice session.
