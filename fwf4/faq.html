What Does Your Training Look Like?

It has taken me several months just to acclimate to movement. Now that I am nearly ready, the season has closed. Because I have been training relentlessly, I know by the time next season opens, I will be ready for a sprint! My goal is to be able to reach the required distance for each sport and then incorporate what is called brick training. I am taking Meredith Atwood to heart when she said The... journey is about making bold moves and strong decisions, but that does not necessarily mean rushing into goals either. The following is a table demonstrating my typical training week.
