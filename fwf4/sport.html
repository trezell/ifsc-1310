About the Sport

The sport that I fell in love with is called triathlon. Some may have heard of the infamous Ironman Race. Now, I may never do a full-on Ironman, however, I can assure you I am well on my way to a sprint. So what exactly is a triathlon? It is a racing event in which a person partakes in 3 major sporting events in one sitting. The events vary by distance. The typical race and the order of the activities are as follows:

  1. Swimming
  2. Biking
  3. Running

There are 4 main types of triathlon

  • Sprint
      • Swim Distance:
      500-900 meters
      Bike Distance:
      10-18 miles
      Run Distance:
      3.1 miles (5k)
  • Olympic
      • Swim Distance:
      0.9 miles
      Bike Distance:
      25 miles
      Run Distance:
      6.2 miles (10k)
  • Half Ironman (70.3)
      • Swim Distance:
      1.2 miles
      Bike Distance:
      56 miles
      Run Distance:
      13.1 miles (half marathon)
  • Ironman (140.6)
      • Swim Distance:
      2.4 miles
      Bike Distance:
      112 miles
      Run Distance:
      26.2 miles (full marathon)
